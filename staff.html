Staff Portal

If you are NOT a Deakin Staff or Admin member, please access the Student or Guest portals or return to the home page.

Here, staff members can access HR Information, Payroll Documents and Teaching Schedules

HR Information

Faculty and Staff Directory: This section typically provides a searchable directory of faculty and staff members at the university. You can usually search by name, department, or position to find contact information for specific individuals. Faculty Profiles: Many university staff pages include individual profiles for faculty members, which may include information such as: Name and title Contact information (email, phone number, office location) Academic qualifications and degrees Areas of expertise or research interests Professional affiliations and memberships Publications and research projects

Teaching Schedules

Core teaching hours: Monday to Friday from 8 am to 10 pm but in the case of undergraduate courses, these will normally be scheduled from 8 am to 8 pm and in the case of postgraduate courses, these will normally be scheduled using a mix of day and evening classes up to 10 pm.
